Monthly detail — TAO & USD

Read "Sold" asalpha unstaked → swapped to TAO, not a wallet transfer. These are not coldkey token transfers — transfers out of both owner wallets are ~zero since dTAO. "Sold" = alpha taken out of the owner's staked position. Under dTAO the only way to exit an alpha position is to remove_stake, which swaps the alpha into TAO through the subnet's pool at the prevailing alpha price — so every figure here is alpha swapped into TAO, valued at that price. Not confirmed as fiat/exchange sales: the TAO proceeds did not leave via transfers and free balances are ~0, so they were most likely redeployed (restaked) rather than cashed out. Treat this as sell-side pressure on the alpha token, not confirmed fiat realisation.

What the wallet says

Method. Owner emission = 0.18 × monthly change in subnet total_alpha (the 18% owner cut), valued at month-end alpha and TAO prices — validated against realised owner-hotkey accrual on SN25. Sold = emission earned − change in the owner hotkey's staked position, valued the same way. A negative "sold" (green) means the position grew by more than the owner cut that month — the owner hotkey also earns validator rewards and can restake, so it accumulated rather than sold. Only the one registered owner coldkey per subnet is traced. Realised USD is timing-dependent because alpha prices fell sharply. Verify against Taostats before external use.

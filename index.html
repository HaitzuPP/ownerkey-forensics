Monthly detail — TAO & USD

Read "Sold" asalpha unstaked → swapped to TAO, not a wallet transfer. Under dTAO the only way to exit an alpha position is to remove_stake, which swaps alpha into TAO through the subnet's pool at the prevailing price — so each figure is alpha swapped into TAO, valued at that price. It is not a coldkey token transfer (those are ~zero) and not confirmed as a fiat/exchange sale — the proceeds were most likely redeployed. "Retained" is the owner coldkey's total staked alpha across all its hotkeys (via account history), and each series starts the month the owner wallet went live; earlier emissions on re-registered netuids belonged to prior owners and are excluded. First live month is a baseline (position established, not a sale).

What the wallet says

Method. Owner emission = 0.18 × monthly change in subnet total_alpha (the 18% owner cut), valued at month-end alpha and TAO prices. Retained = the owner coldkey's total staked-alpha value across all hotkeys, from Taostats account history, converted to alpha at each month's price. Sold = emission earned − change in retained. A negative "sold" (green) = the position grew faster than the owner cut (validator rewards / restaking / buying) = accumulation, not a sale. Each subnet's series begins when its owner wallet first holds stake; for re-registered netuids (Ditto, Minos, SCORE) earlier emissions accrued to prior owners and are excluded. Realised USD is timing-dependent as alpha prices moved. Verify against Taostats before external use.
